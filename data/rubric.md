# Evaluation Rubric

## Scoring Scale: 1-10

---

## CONCEPT (explain a tactical/technical concept)

A good answer must include:
- Accurate and clear definition of the concept (3 points)
- A concrete example of how it works in a real match or team (3 points)
- Simple language that a non-expert could understand (2 points)
- Mention of when/why a team would use it (2 points)

---

## HISTORY (factual questions)

A good answer must include:
- Correct and precise facts including exact numbers and dates (5 points)
- Relevant context around the fact (3 points)
- No hallucinated statistics or names (2 points)

---

## COMPARE (compare two things)

A good answer must include:
- Clear explanation of each side separately (3 points)
- Direct comparison with specific differences (4 points)
- Mention of when each option is preferred (3 points)

---

## ANALYSIS (why did something happen)

A good answer must include:
- A clear main argument/thesis (3 points)
- At least 2 supporting reasons with a specific real-world example (match, season, or player) (4 points)
- Acknowledgment of complexity or alternative views (3 points)
